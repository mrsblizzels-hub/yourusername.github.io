Gymnastics Facts

  1. Gymnastics levels
    • Compulsary levels 2-5
    • Optional 6-10
    • Jr Elite (if younger than 15)
    • Senior Elite (Older than 15)
  2. Simone Biles Facts (Greatest Gymnast of All Time)
    • Simone Biles has won a total of 32 Olympic and World Championship medals, making her the most decorated American gymnast in history.
    • Several gymnastics skills are named after her because she was the first woman to perform them in competition, like the “Biles” on floor, vault and beam.
    • Simone uses her platform to speak out on issues like mental health, racial equality, and empowering young athletes.
  3. Skills named After Simone Biles
    • Vault: Yerchenko Double Pike
    • Floor: Triple Double, Double layout half
    • Beam: Double Double beam dismount
